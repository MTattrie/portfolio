About Me

That's me. Mark Tattrie: A 24 year old software developer that enjoys music, video-games and being active.

After completing high-school I wasn’t quite sure what I wanted to pursue in life. I ended up going to Langara College taking a variety of courses in History, Japanese Culture, Kinesiology and Computer Science. It was here that I found out I had a passion for Computer Science. I loved the problem-solving, the logic, and the satisfaction of finally fixing those issues you’ve been struggling on for hours to complete a project. After getting a taste, I wanted to learn more. I decided to continue my education by enrolling into BCIT’s Computer Systems Technology program, graduating in April 2017. If you want to learn more about my BCIT experience, check out my blog post about it.
